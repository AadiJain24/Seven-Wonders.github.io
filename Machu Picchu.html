Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is a 15th-century Inca citadel.
It is located in southern Peru on a 2,430-meter (7,970 ft) mountain ridge.
It is located in the Machupicchu District within Urubamba
Province above the Sacred Valley, which is 80 kilometers (50 mi)
northwest of Cusco.
